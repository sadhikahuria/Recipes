Margarita

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 shot Tequila
  • 2 tablespoons Freshly-squeezed lime juice
  • 1 shot Orange liqueur
  • 1/2 tablespoon Agave syrup
  • 2-3 tablespoons Kosher salt
  • 1 slice of lime
  • 1/2 cup of ice

Steps

  1. Prep your glass by simply running a juice lime wedge around the rim of the glass and dip the rim in Kosher salt. Set the glass aside.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, combine the tequila, lime juice, orange liqueur, and a few ice cubes. Shake for 20-25 seconds until chilled.
  3. Give the mix a taste. Add anything desired.
  4. Place 2 ice cubes in our glass from before. Strain our mixture and slowly add it over the ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with a slice of lime.
  6. Enjoy!
